About Us

Kuji! Magazine exists as a hub to empower artists, makers, and boundary pushers alike and create an unparalleled environment for the aspirational to be able to promote their work and gain inspiration, a platform for the unheard to find community and constructive audiences for their ideations, and for the marginalized to seek patronage and collaboration in hopes of becoming the infrastructure for higher quality engagement, optimized utility of service, and proliferation of ideas in a generation to come. Based in the DMV, its founder is a soon-to-be renown music producer & artist, web3 developer, game designer, visual artist, and writer with an idealist vision for a better connected world with greater realization of individual potential. We are always seeking likeminded creative minds to join the team. With a variety of upcoming projects in the domain of digital software, service platforms, and interactive games as well as physical merchandise, clothing, and organized events, we hope you can find something of value in the vast array of things we have in store the near future!

@kujimagazine is a design and culture digital magazine (+the option of a shipped physical copy) independently published with a showcase of talented multimedia artists alongside a collection of writings on topics ranging from blockchain tech, governance & the future of decentralized orgs (DAOs), futurism & thought pieces on the trajectory of human civilization and its emergent dialectics, interviews and features of connected prominent figures, and much more. Each issue will be themed differently with the 1st specified toward cryptography-based solutions for current world problems. We would love to feature content and perspectives from any interested creative minds, so feel free to reach out to us via direct message. Our magazine is seeking to explore the complex layered discussions of each topic by encouraging an interdisciplinary discourse and eventually creating a passionate link between philosophy, code, design, videography and many other disciplines.
